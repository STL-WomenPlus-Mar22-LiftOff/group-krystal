How to add font to VSC:

follow this link:
https://www.dafont.com/amsterdam-4.font

download to your desktop or wherever you have your folder for trackfully 

extract the file (truetype font file)

open file, and click install (might be different for macs)

then go to VSC > file >  preferences > setting > Font Family 

add amsterdam to the list 

you may need to restart VSC and/or computer to completely get it to work

also, here is the link to follow the directions if I am not clear.
