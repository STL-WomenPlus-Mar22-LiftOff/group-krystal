About Trackfully

Trackfully began as nothing more than an idea to make tracking our daily habits or symptoms easier.

You don't want to hassle with spreadsheets and bulky apps when you're not feeling well or trying to make better choices. Trackfully offers a simple and streamlined application where you can easily track what you want to track and see a month's worth of data all in one place.

When you register for a free and secure account, you gain access to a personalized symptom management page and dashboard. Here you can customize what 3 symptoms or habits you would like to track. With the user-friendly daiily tracker form, you can select a date, a symptom and rate it on a scale of 1-10. Your daily data for up to 3 symptoms appearing in a simple line graph on the dashboard gives you a quick overview of how you've been doing. It can help pinpoint problem areas and give insights into what might have caused trouble during specific days or weeks.

About Us

We utilized components we were taught in class as well as learned about and implemented new components to build this project.
